# Template: Cold Outreach Email

**Subject:** [Specific, relevant subject — avoid clickbait]

---

Hi [NAME],

[1-sentence observation or genuine compliment about their work — show you've done your homework.]

[1-2 sentences on who you are and why that's relevant to them — not a resume dump.]

[1-sentence specific ask — make it easy to say yes or no.]

[Optional: one concrete proof point — result, link, or mutual connection.]

[NAME]

---

**Tips:**
- Keep total length under 100 words.
- The subject line should be specific, not clever.
- The ask should require less than 10 minutes from them.
